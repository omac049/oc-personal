Technical SEO Checklistâ€‹

Complete technical foundation audit covering crawlability, indexability, site speed, mobile optimization, and structured data implementation.

Best For:

Key Areas Covered:

  • Core Web Vitals optimization
  • Mobile-first indexing compliance

Key Areas Covered:

  • Title tag optimization
  • Meta description best practices
  • Header structure (H1-H6)
  • Content quality & relevance
  • Internal linking strategy
  • Image optimization

Systematic framework for evaluating existing content performance, identifying gaps, and prioritizing improvement opportunities.

Best For:

  • Quarterly content reviews
  • Website consolidation projects
  • Traffic recovery initiatives
  • Content strategy planning

Key Areas Covered:

  • Content inventory creation
  • Performance metrics analysis
  • Quality & relevance assessment
  • Gap identification
  • Action prioritization

Location-based optimization guide for Google Business Profile, local citations, reviews, and geo-targeted content.

  • Local content creation

ğŸ“ How to Use These Checklistsâ€‹

1. Choose the Right Checklistâ€‹

Select the checklist that aligns with your current optimization goals. For comprehensive audits, work through multiple checklists systematically.

2. Customize for Your Needsâ€‹

Adapt these checklists to your specific:

  • Industry requirements
  • Technical capabilities
  • Resource constraints
  • Business objectives

3. Track Your Progressâ€‹

  • Download or print checklists for offline use
  • Use project management tools to track completion
  • Document findings and recommendations
  • Set realistic timelines for implementation

4. Iterate & Improveâ€‹

SEO is ongoing. Schedule regular checklist reviews:

  • Technical SEO: Quarterly
  • On-Page SEO: Monthly for priority pages
  • Content Audit: Quarterly or bi-annually
  • Local SEO: Monthly for GMB, quarterly for citations

  • Broken internal links

Track these metrics to validate your optimization efforts:

Technical Improvements:
